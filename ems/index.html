Ethnic Minority Students

Introduction

Welcome to our research project, “Perceived Value-Based Student Satisfaction among Ethnic Minorities in Hong Kong Tertiary Academic Institutions.”

The goal of this research is to investigate the factors influencing perceived value-based student satisfaction among ethnic minorities in Hong Kong’s tertiary academic institutions. By understanding these factors, we aim to identify areas for improvement and develop strategies to enhance the educational experiences and satisfaction of ethnic minority students.
